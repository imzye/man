NAME
       scramble - A word finding game.

SYNOPSIS
       hangman

DESCRIPTION
       Test your word knowledge, and spelling skills, with this anagram game.
       You are given 6
        letters to create as many words as you can in the time alotted and you
       must find at
        least half of the words possible before you can go to the next level.
