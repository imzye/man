NAME
       simplech - lets you play checkers against the computer.



SYNOPSIS
       simplech



DESCRIPTION
       simplech first asks, whether you want to play the black or the white
       stones and how long the program may calculate its moves. It then starts
       a game; When its your move, you may either enter a letter from the
       list, or z to abort the current game or t to run some benchmarks. At
       the end of a game you are asked to play again or to quit.

       The letters used to draw the board are: - for an empty node, b for a
       black man, B a black king, w a white man, W a white king.
