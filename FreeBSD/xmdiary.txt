NAME
       xdaclunix - control access to your UNIX XDiary databases


SYNOPSIS
       xdaclunix [-usage] [xdiaryOption ...] [toolkitOption ...]


DESCRIPTION
       xdaclunix allows you to control access to your XDiary databases when
       XDiary is installed as a group calendar.


OPTIONS
       All standard Xt Intrinsics toolkit options are supported.  In addition,
       xdaclunix supports several other options which are described in the
       XDiary on-line help.


FILES
       $HOME/Diary/dbAccess


SEE ALSO
       The built in help in XDiary.
