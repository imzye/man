NAME
       XGalaga++ - X11 classic vertical scrolling shoot'em up

SYNOPSIS
       xgalaga++

DESCRIPTION
       XGalaga++ is a classic vertical scrolling shoot'em up: fire at aliens,
       collect bonuses and survive as long as you can.

KEYBOARD COMMANDS
       1,2,3,4,5
              Resize the window to 640x480, 800x600, 1024x768, 1280x1024, or
              1600x1200.

       h      Toggle high scores and menu.

       p      Pause.

       q      End game, or quit XGalaga++ (from menu).

       s      Start game

       space  Fire.

       left/right arrow
              Move vessel left/right.

       up/down arrow
              Scroll high scores up/down.

       -/+    Decrease/increase details (use a bit less CPU and memory).

       r      Increment video refresh rate by 5 Hz in range 50 - 120 Hz.

ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES
       XGAL_PSEUDO - Player pseudonym, used for high scores.
