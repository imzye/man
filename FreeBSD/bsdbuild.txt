     man2mediawiki â€“ Generate MediaWiki articles from manual pages

SYNOPSIS
     man2mediawiki [-u -username] [category]

DESCRIPTION
     The man2mediawiki tool uses formatted manual page source as generated by
     man2wiki(1) to generate (and update) a category of MediaWiki articles
     (via MediaWiki::API(3)).

     The options are as follows:

     -u      Specify username to log in to the wiki.  The password will be
             asked interactively and stored in ~/.man2mediawiki.

SEE ALSO
     man2wiki(1), MediaWiki::API(3), build.common.mk(5), build.man.mk(5)
