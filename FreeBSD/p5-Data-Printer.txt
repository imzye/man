NAME
       DDP - Data::Printer shortcut for faster debugging

SYNOPSIS
         use DDP; p $my_data;

DESCRIPTION
       Tired of typing "use Data::Printer" every time? "DDP" lets you quickly
       call your favorite variable dumper!

       It behaves exactly like Data::Printer - it is, indeed, just an alias to
       it :)

       Happy debugging!

SEE ALSO
       Data::Printer
