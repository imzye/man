     wwl â€“ Maidenhead locator utility

SYNOPSIS
     This program does Maidenhead calculations of distance and bearing for any
     two Maidenhead locators given on the command line; Alternatively the
     program may be used to return the Latitude and Longitude of a single
     Maidenhead grid square when invoked as locator.

EXAMPLES
     wwl fn25di jo55ei
     qrb: 5810 kilometers, azimuth: 46 degrees

     wwl jo55 ec41
     qrb: 15996 kilometers, azimuth: 216 degrees

     locator FN25di
     Locator    : FN25DI
     Coordinates: Long: (W) -75.75 Lat : (N) 45.3333

     locator jo55ei
     Locator    : JO55EI
     Coordinates: Long: (E) 10.33 Lat : (N) 55.3333

     locator ec41
     Locator    : EC41AA
     Coordinates: Long: (W) -92.00 Lat : (S) -69.0000
