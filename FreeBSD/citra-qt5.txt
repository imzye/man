SYNOPSIS

DESCRIPTION
     Citra is an experimental open-source Nintendo 3DS emulator/debugger.

     citra-qt is the Qt implementation.

FILES
     $XDG_DATA_HOME/citra-emu
             Emulator storage.

     $XDG_CONFIG_HOME/citra-emu
             Configuration files.
