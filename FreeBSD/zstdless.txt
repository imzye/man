NAME
       zstdless - view zstandard-compressed files

SYNOPSIS
       zstdless [flags] [file ...]

DESCRIPTION
       zstdless runs less (1) on files or stdin, if no files argument is
       given, after decompressing them with zstdcat (1).

SEE ALSO
       zstd (1)
