SYNOPSIS

DESCRIPTION
     The ow module implements the Dallas Semiconductor 1-Wire bus.  It
     attaches to the owc(4) driver, which implements the low-level signaling
     of the 1-Wire bus.

SEE ALSO
     ow_temp(4), owc(4), owll(9), own(9)
