NAME
       tartube - GUI front-end for youtube-dl and yt-dlp

SYNOPSIS
       tartube

DESCRIPTION
       Tartube is a GUI front-end for youtube-dl, yt-dlp and other compatible
       video downloades, written in Python 3 / Gtk 3. It's a convenient way to
       download videos from YouTube and hundreds of other sites (or just to
       fetch a list of those videos, if you prefer).

OPTIONS
       The tartube executable does not take any options.

SEE ALSO
       youtube-dl(8)

BUGS
       No known bugs.
