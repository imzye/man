DESCRIPTION
     On UEFI systems, loader.efi loads the kernel.  It is installed into the
     ESP (EFI System Partition), either in the default location of
     /efi/boot/bootXXX.efi within the ESP, in the FreeBSD reserved area as
     /efi/freebsd/loader.efi within the ESP , or within the system as
     /boot/loader.efi.  boot1.efi(8) is used to load loader.efi when it is
     placed within the system.
