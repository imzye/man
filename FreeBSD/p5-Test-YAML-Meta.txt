NAME
       Test::YAML::Meta - Validate your CPAN META.yml files.

DESCRIPTION
       This module has now been replaced by Test::CPAN::Meta::YAML. Please see
       that distribution for further details.

ABSTRACT
       A test module to validate a META.yml file.

WARNING
       This distribution will be retired at the end of 2015. Please replace
       your references to this module with Test::CPAN::Meta::YAML.

BUGS, PATCHES & FIXES
       There are no known bugs at the time of this release. However, if you
       spot a bug or are experiencing difficulties that are not explained
       within the POD documentation, please send an email to barbie@cpan.org
       or submit a bug to the RT system
       (http://rt.cpan.org/Public/Dist/Display.html?Name=Test-YAML-Meta).
       However, it would help greatly if you are able to pinpoint problems or
       even supply a patch.

       Fixes are dependent upon their severity and my availability. Should a
       fix not be forthcoming, please feel free to (politely) remind me.
