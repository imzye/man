NAME
       patchage - Graphically connect JACK and ALSA Audio and MIDI ports


SYNOPSIS
       patchage [OPTION]...


DESCRIPTION
       Patchage provides a graphical interface to connect JACK and ALSA inputs
       and outputs.  Each application is represented on a canvas as a
       “module”, with inputs on the left and outputs on the right.  Modules
       can be arranged manually or automatically to have a clear display of
       the current setup.


OPTIONS
       -A, --no-alsa
              Do not automatically attach to ALSA.


       -h, --help
              Print the command line options and exit.


       -J, --no-jack
              Do not automatically attach to JACK.


       -V, --version
              Print version information and exit.
