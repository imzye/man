NAME
       krb5_principal_intro - The principal handing functions.  A Kerberos
       principal is a email address looking string that contains to parts
       separeted by a @. The later part is the kerbero realm the principal
       belongs to and the former is a list of 0 or more components. For
       example


       lha@SU.SE
       host/hummel.it.su.se@SU.SE
       host/admin@H5L.ORG


       See the library functions here: Heimdal Kerberos 5 principal functions
