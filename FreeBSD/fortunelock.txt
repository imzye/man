NAME
       fortunelock - locks virtual terminal while displaying fortune cookies


SYNOPSIS
       fortunelock


DESCRIPTION
       Fortunelock is a "keep it simple, stupid" program to lock a terminal
       with while displaying fortune cookies from the fortune(6) program.

       It was designed to be as small and simple as possible and therefore
       does not support PAM, NIS, shadow passwords or even regular passwords:
       the user must enter a lock password to lock the terminal.


SEE ALSO
       fortune(6)
