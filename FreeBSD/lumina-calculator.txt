NAME
     lumina-calculator – full scientific calculator with a simple interface.


SYNOPSIS
     lumina-calculator



DESCRIPTION
     lumina-calculator is a simple scientific calculator interface.  It has
     exponential, trigonometric, and logarithmic functions.  It can also
     calculate percentages, square roots, and hyperbolic trigonometric
     functions.  The utility keeps a history of previous calculations.  This
     history can be saved as a .txt file.


FILES
     /usr/local/bin/lumina-calculator
