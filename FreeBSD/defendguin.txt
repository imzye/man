NAME
       defendguin - A bidirectionally scrolling space game based on Defender.

SYNTAX
       defendguin [options]

DESCRIPTION
       Aliens are attacking earth, kidnapping little penguinoids and
       converting them into mutants.  Helping them on their way are some other
       nasty alien ships, of which there are plenty.  Defeat them and save the
       penguinoids.

OPTIONS
       --disable-sound
              Disables sound and music.

       --fullscreen
              Runs in fullscreen mode, if possible.

       --help Output help information and exit.

       --version
              Output version information and exit.

       --usage
              Displays acceptable command-line options.

FILES
       /usr/local/share/defendguin/ - Sound, music and graphics data.
