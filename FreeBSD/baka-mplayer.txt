NAME
       baka-mplayer - A free and open source, cross-platform, libmpv based
       multimedia player.


SYNOPSIS
       baka-mplayer [file|-]


DESCRIPTION
       Baka-MPlayer is a free and open source, cross-platform, libmpv based
       multimedia player. Its simple design reflects the idea for an
       uncluttered, simple, and enjoyable environment for watching tv shows.

       Baka-MPlayer is a graphical front-end to mpv. It is mostly self-
       explanatory and has little need for a manual.


EXAMPLES
       baka-mplayer
       baka-mplayer myvideo.mkv
       cat myvideo.mkv | baka-mplayer -


SEE ALSO
       mpv
