NAME
       gpredict - real-time satellite tracking and orbit prediction program


SYNOPSIS
       gpredict [OPTION]...


DESCRIPTION
       Gpredict is a real-time satellite tracking and orbit prediction
       program. It can track an unlimited number of satellites and display the
       satellite data in various visualisation modules like lists, maps and
       polar plots. Gpredict can also provide detailed information about
       upcoming satellite passes relative to one or more ground stations.
       Gpredict can perform Doppler tuning and antenna rotator tracking via
       hamlib. The complete user manual, as well as other documentation, is
       available from http://gpredict.oz9aec.net/documents.php
