NAME
       B::Size2 - Measure size of Perl OPs and SVs

SYNOPSIS
         use B ();
         use B::Size2 ();

         say B::svref_2object($value)->size;

DESCRIPTION
       B::Size2 is a fork of B::Size 0.09 for maintainance.
