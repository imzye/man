NAME
     apc – xforms Auto Payment Calculator

SYNOPSIS
     apc [xforms-options]

DESCRIPTION
     The apc program is a xforms Auto Payment Calculator that you can use to
     help you decide on a auto payment purchase.

SEE ALSO
     xforms(5)
