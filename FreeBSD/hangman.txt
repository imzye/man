NAME
       hangman - A word guessing game.

SYNOPSIS
       hangman [OPTIONS] ...

DESCRIPTION
       Challenge your spelling skills in the old western style. You have seven
       chances to get it right or you're hung! Get it right and we'll let you
       try again, until you get it wrong. But we will give you another chance
       if you get it wrong.

OPTIONS
       -d DICTIONARY_NAME
              used to load an alternate dictionary. By default
              /usr/share/dict/words is used.
