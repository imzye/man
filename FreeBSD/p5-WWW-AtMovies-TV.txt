NAME
       WWW::AtMovies::TV - retrieve TV information from
       http://www.atmovies.com.tw/

VERSION
       Version 0.04

SYNOPSIS
           use WWW::AtMovies::TV;

           my $foo = WWW::AtMovies::TV->new();
           $foo->now;
           $foo->next;

FUNCTIONS
   new
   now
       retrieve "now on" information

   next
       retrieve "next on" information
