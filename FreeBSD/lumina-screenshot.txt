NAME
     lumina-screenshot – a utility to take and save screenshots of the
     desktop.


SYNOPSIS
     lumina-screenshot



DESCRIPTION
     lumina-screenshot opens the graphical utility and takes a screenshot.
     Then, it can view, crop, zoom, and save the screenshot.  There is also an
     option to open the screenshot with a different application.  There are
     options to capture the entire session, a single screen, a single window,
     or a selected area.  By default, the utility is bound to the
     "PrintScreen" keyboard button.


FILES
     /usr/local/bin/lumina-screenshot
