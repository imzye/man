NAME
     koi2koi – cyrillic charset auto converter

SYNOPSIS
     koi2koi [<infile] [>outfile]

DESCRIPTION
     The koi2koi utility tries to autoconvert input stream from stdout from
     any of the following encodings: koi8, cp866, cp1251, iso8859-5,
     maccyrillic.  koi2koi writes output to stdout.
