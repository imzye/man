SLICK-GREETER-CHECK_HIDPI(1)                   FreeBSD General Commands Manual

NAME
       slick-greeter-check-hidpi - distro agnostic LightDM greeter

SYNOPSIS
       slick-greeter-check-hidpi [ OPTIONS ]

DESCRIPTION
       Slick Greeter Check HiDPI simple check to see if the current resolution
       is less that 1500px in height (returns 1) or returns 2 if the
       resolution is considered as HiDPI.

OPTIONS
