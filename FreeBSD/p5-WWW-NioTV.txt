NAME
       WWW::NioTV - retrieve TV information from http://www.niotv.com/

VERSION
       Version 0.04

SYNOPSIS
           use WWW::NioTV;

           my $tv = WWW::NioTV->new;
           $tv->now;
           $tv->next;

FUNCTIONS
   new
       create a WWW::NioTV object

   now
       retrieve "now on" tv information

   next
       retrieve "next on" tv information
