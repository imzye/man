     getpmac â€“ print process-related MAC labels

SYNOPSIS
     getpmac [-l list,of,labels] [-p pid]

DESCRIPTION
     The getpmac utility prints the text representation of the MAC label
     associated with the specified process.  If no process is specified, it
     prints the label associated with its own process.
