SND_VIBES(4)           FreeBSD Kernel Interfaces Manual           SND_VIBES(4)

NAME
     snd_vibes – S3 SonicVibes bridge device driver

SYNOPSIS
     To compile this driver into the kernel, place the following lines in your
     kernel configuration file:

           device sound
           device snd_vibes

     Alternatively, to load the driver as a module at boot time, place the
     following line in loader.conf(5):

           snd_vibes_load="YES"

DESCRIPTION
     The snd_vibes bridge driver allows the generic audio driver, sound(4), to
     attach audio devices based on the S3 SonicVibes chipset.

HARDWARE
     The snd_vibes driver supports audio devices based on the following
     chipset:

     •   S3 SonicVibes

SEE ALSO
     sound(4)

HISTORY
     The snd_vibes device driver first appeared in FreeBSD 4.4.
