NAME
     yes – be repetitively affirmative

SYNOPSIS
     yes [expletive]

DESCRIPTION
     The yes utility outputs expletive, or, by default, “y”, forever.

SEE ALSO
     jot(1), seq(1)

HISTORY
     The yes command appeared in Version 7 AT&T UNIX.
