NAME
       Moose::Exception::InvalidAttributeDefinition - MooseX::Extended
       exception for invalid attribute definitions.

VERSION
       version 0.35

WHY NOT MOOSEX?
       This is not called "MooseX::Exception::InvalidAttributeDefinition"
       because Moose::Util's "throw_exception" function assumes that all
       exceptions begin with "Moose::Exception::".
