NAME
       InspIRCd - the stable, high-performance and modular Internet Relay Chat
       Daemon


SYNOPSIS
       inspircd-testssl <hostip> <port> [selfsigned]


OPTIONS
       hostip
              The hostname or IP address to connect to.

       port
              The TCP port to connect to.
       selfsigned Disables checking whether the server certificate is signed
       by a Certificate Authority.


SUPPORT
       IRC support for InspIRCd can be found at
       ircs://irc.teranova.net/inspircd.

       Bug reports and feature requests can be filed at
       https://github.com/inspircd/inspircd/issues.
