NAME
       webgpg - initializes maildir subdirectory gpg

SYNOPSIS
       webgpg MAILDIR

DESCRIPTION
       webgpg creates a new subdirectory in the given MAILDIR and initializes it. Once that subdirectory is created, encryption/decryption in SqWebMail will become available for that account.

       webgpg must be executed by the same UID and GID which owns the MAILDIR.

SEE ALSO
       gpg(1), /usr/share/doc/sqwebmail/GPG.html
