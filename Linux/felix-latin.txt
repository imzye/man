NAME
       felix - A browser for a Latin-French dictionary

SYNOPSIS

       felix

DESCRIPTION
       felix is a program to access Felix Gaffiot's Latin-French dictionary. Entering a Latin word will open the dictionary at the right page and let you zoom and pad over the scanned page of the
       dictionary published in year 1934.
