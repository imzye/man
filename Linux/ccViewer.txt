NAME
       ccViewer - light 3d viewer

SYNOPSIS
       ccViewer files...

DESCRIPTION
       This manual page documents briefly the ccViewer command.

       ccViewer is a light 3d viewer accompaigning CloudCompare.  Press the left mouse button, to rotate your 3d data model in the viewer.  For built-in help, press the F1 key.

SEE ALSO
       CloudCompare(1).
