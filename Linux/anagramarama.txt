NAME
       anagramarama - cute anagram game using SDL

SYNOPSIS
       anagramarama

DESCRIPTION
       anagramarama  is  a simple wordgame in which one tries to guess all the
       different permutations of a scrambled  word  which  form  another  word
       within  the time limit.  Guess the original word and you move on to the
       next level.

OPTIONS
       None, currently.
