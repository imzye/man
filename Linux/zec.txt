NAME
       zec - Z-Shell Empire Client

SYNOPSIS

       zec [country] [representative] [host] [port]

DESCRIPTION
       This manual page documents briefly the zec command.

       zec is a client for the game Empire.

       For more information about Empire, see the Empire website[1].

ARGUMENTS
       Currently, all four arguments are required if you do not have a ~/.zecrc.

       The first is the name of your country.

       The second argument is the representative of your country.

       The third is the resolvable hostname or IP of the server to which you wish to connect.

       The fourth and final is the TCP port number of said server.

       If you do not know these values, contact your game administrator.
