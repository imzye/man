NAME
       pd-iem - IEM flavoured Pure Data

DESCRIPTION
       pd-iem  is  a  distribution of Pure Data (Pd) as used by the Institute of Electronic Music and Acoustics (iem) at the University of Music and Performing Arts Graz, Austria.  It closely follows the
       vanilla flavour of Miller Puckette but includes a few select externals.

       This script starts Pd with a couple of libraries enabled. All arguments passed to it, will be forwarded to Pd.  To get a list of allowable arguments to Pd (pd-iem), type

              pd-iem -help

       and for more documentation either start pd-iem and get help, or consult

       https://git.iem.at/pd/pd-iem
