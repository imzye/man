NAME
       retweet - simple Twitter bot to retweet

SYNOPSIS
       retweet retweet.ini

DESCRIPTION
       Retweet automatically retweets tweets from a Twitter account to another one.

EXAMPLES
       Launch Retweet

       $ retweet retweet.ini
