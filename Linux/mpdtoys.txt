NAME
       mprandomwalk - play random bits of all queued songs

SYNOPSIS
       mprandomwalk [maxduration] [host]

DESCRIPTION
       mprandomwalk plays random segments of randomly chosen songs in the playlist.

       The maxduration can control the maximum number of seconds of a song to play at a time.

       If the hostname is omitted, the MPD_HOST environment variable will be used.
