NAME
       projectx - DVB Demuxing utility

DESCRIPTION
       projectx is an application designed to clean up and demux DVB streams.  European digital radio & television uses the DVB standard to broadcast its data.  Project X gives you a look behind the
       transmissions and tries its best to handle and repair many stream types and shows what went wrong on reception.

AUTHOR
       projectx is by dvb.matt
