NAME
       x2goejectcd - CD/DVD bay eject tool for X2Go Thin Clients.

SYNOPSIS
       x2goejectcd

DESCRIPTION
       x2goejectcd is used to eject the CD/DVD bay of optical discs built into X2Go thin clients.

OPTIONS
       x2goejectcd has no known options.
