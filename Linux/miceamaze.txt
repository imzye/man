NAME
       MiceAmaze - video game with mice in a maze

SYNOPSIS
       miceamaze [OPTIONS]

DESCRIPTION
       MiceAmaze  is a free video game that features a maze with mice and snakes.  The goal is to bring the most mice to your house by placing arrows on the maze floor. But beware the snakes that want to
       eat them!

OPTIONS
       --window
              start in window mode (not fullscreen)

       --fullscreen
              start in fullscreen mode
