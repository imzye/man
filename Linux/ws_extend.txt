NAME
       ws_extend - extend a workspace

SYNOPSIS
       ws_extend WORKSPACE DAYS

DESCRIPTION
       Extend a workspace to last until DAYS in the future.  This is a convenience wrapper around ws_allocate -x

AUTHOR
       Written by Holger Berger

SEE ALSO
