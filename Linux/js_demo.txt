NAME
       js_demo - Joystick demo application for FlightGear

SYNOPSIS
       js_demo

DESCRIPTION
       js_demo  is a FlightGear utility that shows detected joysticks and pro‐
       vides a way to monitor them as their axes are moved  or  their  buttons
       are pressed.

EXIT STATUS
       js_demo always exits with 0.
