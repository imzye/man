NAME
       bless - graphical hexadecimal Gtk# editor

SYNOPSIS
       bless [FILE]...

DESCRIPTION
       Edit the FILEs as a sequence of bytes, allowing read/write, search, pattern finding, efficient query-replace, multi-tabbing, customized data-views, plugins, and many other features.
