NAME
       jpeg_extract - jpeg extractor

SYNOPSIS
       jpeg_extract <filename>

DESCRIPTION
       jpeg_extractor is used by fiwalk, provided by TSK (The Sleuth Kit) to manipulate JPG files.

AUTHOR
       The Sleuth Kit was written by Brian Carrier <carrier@sleuthkit.org>.
