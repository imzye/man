NAME
       mothur -  sequence analysis suite for research on microbiota

SYNOPSIS
       mothur [ batchfile ]

DESCRIPTION
       This manual page documents briefly the mothur command.

       Mothur  seeks  to  develop  a  single piece of open-source, expandable software to fill the bioinformatics needs of the microbial ecology community. It has incorporated the functionality of dotur,
       sons, treeclimber, s-libshuff, unifrac, and much more. In addition to improving the flexibility of these algorithms, a number of other features including calculators and visualization  tools  were
       added.
