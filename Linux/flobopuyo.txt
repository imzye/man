NAME
       flobopuyo - A clone of the Japanese PuyoPuyo game

SYNOPSIS
       flobopuyo

DESCRIPTION
       The goal of flobopuyo is to make groups of four or more Puyos (coloured bubbles) to make them explode and send bad ghost Puyos to your oponent.  You win the game if your oponent reaches the top of
       the board. You can play against computer or another human.
       The game has nice graphics and animations as well as music and sound effects.

OPTIONS
       None.
