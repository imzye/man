NAME
       sasm - simple crossplatform IDE for NASM, GAS and FASM assembly languages

SYNOPSIS
       sasm files...

DESCRIPTION
       This manual page documents briefly the sasm command.

       sasm is a simple crossplatform IDE for NASM, MASM, GAS and FASM assembly languages

       nasm(1), fasm(1), as(1), gss(1), intel2gas(1).
