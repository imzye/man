NAME
       ssimulacra2 - ssimulacra2

SYNOPSIS
       ssimulacra2 orig.png distorted.png

DESCRIPTION
   Returns a score in range -inf..100, which correlates to subjective visual quality:
              30 = low quality (p10 worst output of mozjpeg -quality 30) 50 = medium quality (average output of cjxl -q 40 or mozjpeg -quality 40,

              p10 output of cjxl -q 50 or mozjpeg -quality 60)

       70 = high quality (average output of cjxl -q 70 or mozjpeg -quality 70,
              p10 output of cjxl -q 75 or mozjpeg -quality 80)

       90 = very high quality (impossible to distinguish from original at 1:1,
              average output of cjxl -q 90 or mozjpeg -quality 90)
