NAME
       geary - lightweight email client designed for the GNOME desktop

DESCRIPTION
       geary is an email reader for GNOME designed to let you read your email quickly and effortlessly. Its interface is based on conversations, so you can easily read an entire discussion without having
       to click from message to message.
