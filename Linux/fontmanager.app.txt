NAME
       FontManager - manage your fonts in GNUstep

SYNOPSIS
       FontManager

DESCRIPTION
       This manual page documents briefly the FontManager command.

       FontManager is a GNUstep program that lets you easily manage your installed fonts.

       mknfonts(1), update-nfonts(1).
