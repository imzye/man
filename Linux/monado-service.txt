
NAME
       monado-service — Device and composition service for Monado-based feaetures

SYNOPSIS
       monado-service

DESCRIPTION
       monado-service  is  the  service  module  of  Monado that provides hardware access and composition.  It may be manually started, or allowed to auto-start through systemd socket activation via mon‐
       ado.socket and monado.service.

       It takes no arguments, and detects whether it was started by socket activation automatically.
