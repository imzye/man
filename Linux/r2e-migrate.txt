NAME
       r2e-migrate - Migrate your data from rss2email 2.x to rss2email 3.x.

USAGE
              r2e-migrate

       Please note that you may still have to adapt your configuration.  See r2e(1).

FILES
       2.x versions used to store configuration in ~/.rss2email/config.py and data in ~/.rss2email/feeds.dat.

       Now 3.x stores its configuration in $XDG_CONFIG_HOME/rss2email.cfg and data in $XDG_DATA_HOME/rss2email.json.
