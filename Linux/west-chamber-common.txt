       West-chamber â€” xtables extensions for eluding network intrusion detection.

Targets
   CUI
       The CUI target sends obfuscating packets upon incoming SYN to make the connection invisible to third-party intrusion detection.

   UDPENCAP
       Unconditionally and statelessly encapsulate transport layer packets in UDP packets and decapsulate UDP packets.

       --decap protocol
              Decapsulate to this protocol (number).

       --sport port
              Encapsulate with the source port (number).

       --dport port
              Encapsulate with the destination port (number).

   ZHANG
       The ZHANG target sends obfuscating packets upon incoming SYN/ACK to make the connection invisible to third-party intrusion detection.

Matches
   gfw
       The gfw match allows to match on gfw packet fingerprints.

       xtables-addons(8), iptables(8), ip6tables(8)
