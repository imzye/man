NAME
       toobig6

SYNOPSIS
       toobig6 [-u] interface target-ip existing-ip mtu [hop-limit]

       Implants the specified mtu on the target.  If the TTL of the target is not 64, then specify this as the last option.  Option -u will send the TooBig without the spoofed ping6 from existing-ip.
