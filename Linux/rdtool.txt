NAME
       rd2 - converter from RD to other mark-up language.

SYNOPSIS
           rd2 [-r <visitor>] [options] <file>

DESCRIPTION
       rd2 inputs from <file> and outputs into STDOUT. you can choose <visitor> to select output format. For example, use "rd/rd2html-lib.rb" to translate it into HTML, and "rd/rd2man-lib.rb" to tranlate
       it into roff with man macros.

OPTIONS
       please read output of
           % rd2 --help

       and
           % rd2 -r rd/rd2html-lib.rb --help
