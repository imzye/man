NAME
       auralquiz - simple music quiz game using your own music files

SYNOPSIS
       auralquiz

DESCRIPTION
       Auralquiz  is  a  music quiz game that scans a user's selected folder of music and creates a quiz using the tags from the audio files.  A sample of a random song is played and the player must
       answer a question about the sample from either multiple choice buttons or for hardcore difficulty, typing the correct answer.

       Up to 8 players can play in a turn based mode with scoring based on points; more points are given by answering the question faster.
