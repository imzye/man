Detailed Description
       Iterators.

       The iterator is initially NULL until one of globus_hashtable_first() or globus_hashtable_last() has been called. All other calls have no effect on iterator except for globus_hashtable_remove(). If
       the iterator points at the entry being removed, the iterator is moved to the next entry.

       Once an 'end' has been reached with globus_hashtable_next() or globus_hashtable_prev(), the iterator must again be reset with globus_hashtable_first() or globus_hashtable_last() before being used.
