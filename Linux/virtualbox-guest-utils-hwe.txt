       [--timestamp <last timestamp>]
              [--timeout <timeout in ms>

       VBoxControl  sharedfolder             list [--automount] VBoxControl writecoredump VBoxControl writelog [-n|--no-newline] [--] <msg> VBoxControl takesnapshot VBoxControl savestate VBoxControl
