NAME
       tracklength - prints the total length of all tracks

SYNOPSIS
       tracklength [OPTIONS] <track 1> [track 2] ...

DESCRIPTION
       tracklength  takes a list of audio tracks and writes their total length to standard output in hours:minutes:seconds format. If directories are given, tracklength searches them recursively and adds the
       length of any supported audio files found to the total.

OPTIONS
       -h, --help
              show a list of options and exit
