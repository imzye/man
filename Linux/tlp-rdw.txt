NAME
       tlp-rdw - disable Radio Device Wizard temporarily (until reboot).

SYNOPSIS
       tlp-rdw [command]

COMMANDS
       disable
              Disable RDW actions.

       enable Enable RDW actions.

       <none> Show RDW state.

       --version
              Print TLP version.
