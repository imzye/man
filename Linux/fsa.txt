NAME
       fsa - Distance-based alignment of DNA, RNA and proteins

SYNOPSIS
       fsa [options] <sequence file(s)>

DESCRIPTION
       fsa - Distance-based alignment of DNA, RNA and proteins.

       Type 'fsa --help' for command-line options.

       ERROR: Please specify at least one sequence file.
