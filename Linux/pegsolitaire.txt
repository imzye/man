NAME
       pegsolitaire - puzzle game for one player

SYNOPSIS
       pegsolitaire [option...]

DESCRIPTION
       pegsolitaire is an educational puzzle game.

       The  goal of the game is to remove pegs from the board leaving a single peg in the centre hole.  Pegs are removed from the board by making jumps.  You can jump over a peg with another peg horizon‚Äê
       tally or vertically, but not diagonally.

       When you jump over a peg, it is removed from the board.  Multiple jumps with one peg are allowed.
