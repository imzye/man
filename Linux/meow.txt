NAME
       meow - Print ASCII cats to your terminal

SYNOPSIS
       meow [OPTIONS]

DESCRIPTION
       Print ASCII cats to your terminal

OPTIONS
       -c, --count <COUNT>
              How many cats to print [default: 1]

       -l, --literally
              Are you literally this cat?

       -h, --help
              Print help

       -V, --version
              Print version
