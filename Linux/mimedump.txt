NAME
       mimedump - dump out the contents of a parsed MIME message

DESCRIPTION
       Read a MIME stream from the stdin, and dump its contents to the stdout.

SYNOPSIS
           mimedump < mime.msg

AUTHOR
       Andreas Koenig suggested this, and wrote the original code.  Eryq
       perverted the hell out of it.
