NAME
       imagetooth - Paints the faces of the tooth

SYNOPSIS
       imagetooth [options]

DESCRIPTION
       imagetooth ImageTooth paints the faces of the tooth. The tooth's filename represents the faces painted.

OPTIONS
       The filename's characters are:

       w      White

       g      Green

       b      Blue

       n      Black

       r      Red

       t      To extract

       x      Extracted

       And are ordered, beginning on the upper face of the the tooth and moving clockwise ending on the center face.

EXAMPLE
       imagetooth --image wrrgw.png --folder ./
