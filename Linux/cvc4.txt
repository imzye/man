NAME
       cvc4 - the native input language for CVC4

DESCRIPTION
       cvc4 is an automated theorem prover for first-order formulas with respect to background theories of interest.

HISTORY
SEE ALSO
       cvc4(1), libcvc4(3), libcvc4parser(3)

       Additionally, the CVC4 wiki contains useful information about the design and internals of CVC4.  It is maintained at http://cvc4.cs.stanford.edu/wiki/.
