NAME
       xspp - XS++ preprocessor

SYNOPSIS
           xspp [--typemap=typemap.xsp [--typemap=typemap2.xsp]]
                [--xsubpp[=/path/to/xsubpp] [--xsubpp-args="xsubpp args"]
                Foo.xsp

       or

           perl -MExtUtils::XSpp::Cmd -e xspp -- <xspp options and arguments>

DOCUMENTATION
       See ExtUtils::XSpp.
