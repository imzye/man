NAME
       tssntc2getconfig - Runs TPM2 ntc2getconfig

DESCRIPTION
       ntc2getconfig

       Runs NTC2_GetConfig

       [-verify
              Verify results against System P default (default no verify)]

       [-verifylocked
              Also verify that the preconfig is locked

              (default verify not locked)]

       [-p8 or -p9
              Verify Nuvoton TPM for P8 or P9]
