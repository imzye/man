NAME
       ondelay - open device without waiting for carrier

SYNOPSIS
       /usr/sbin/ondelay

DESCRIPTION
       ondelay is invoked by faxaddmodem and probemodem to open a device without waiting for a carrier.

       This manual page was written for the Debian GNU/Linux distribution (but may be used by others), because the original program does not have a manual page.

SEE ALSO
       hylafax(5), faxaddmodem(8), probemodem(8)

AUTHOR
