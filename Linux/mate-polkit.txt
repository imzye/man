NAME
       mate-polkit - MATE PolicyKit-1 executable wrapper

SYNOPSIS
       mate-polkit

DESCRIPTION
       A wrapper for the MATE PolicyKit-1 executable. Make MATE PolicyKit-1 easily accessible on non-MATE desktops (e.g., i3, awesome, ratpoison and such).
