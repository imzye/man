NAME
       icegridgui - The IceGrid graphical administrative utility.

SYNOPSIS
       icegridgui [options]

DESCRIPTION
       The icegridgui utility is a graphical tool for administering an IceGrid domain. Full documentation for icegridgui is available online at:
       "https://doc.zeroc.com/display/Ice37/IceGrid+Admin+Graphical+Tool".
