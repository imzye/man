NAME
       blobwars - platform shooting game

SYNOPSIS
       blobwars [OPTION]

DESCRIPTION
       Since their world was invaded by an alien race, the Blobs have faced a lifetime of war. But now they have a chance to win the war once and for all.

       In  Blob  Wars:  Metal Blob Solid, you take on the role of a fearless Blob agent, Bob. Bob's mission is to infiltrate the various enemy bases around the Blobs' homeworld and rescue as many MIAs as
       possible. But standing in his way are many vicious aliens, other Blobs who have been assimilated and the evil alien leader, Galdov.

OPTIONS
       -fullscreen
              start the game in full screen mode.

       -mono  use mono sound output instead of stereo.

       -noaudio
              runs the game without sounds and music.

       -version
              shows version number and exits.

       --help displays a summary of command line options to standard output, and exits.

MORE INFO
       The Blob Wars: Metal Blob Solid gameplay manual can be found in </usr/share/doc/blobwars/index.html>.
