NAME
       geographiclib-get-magnetic -- a GeographicLib administrative tool

DESCRIPTION
       geographiclib-get-magnetic downloads and installs the magnetic datasets
       used  by  GeographicLib,  <https://geographiclib.sourceforge.io>.   For
       documentation, supply the -h option:

           geographiclib-get-magnetic -h
