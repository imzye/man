NAME
       shpfix - fix nulls and inconsistencies in shapefiles

SYNOPSIS
       shpfix in_shp_file out_shp_file [record# to blank]

OPTIONS
       in_shp_file
              the name of an existing shapefile.

       out_shp_file
              the name of the new fixed shapefile that will be created.

EXAMPLE
       shpfix broken fixed
