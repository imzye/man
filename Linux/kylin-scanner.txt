NAME
       kylin-scanner - The best GUI frontend for Scanning

SYNOPSIS
       kylin-scanner

DESCRIPTION
       Kylin Scanner is an interface-friendly scanning software developed with Qt5,
              using  SANE(1)  as  its  backend.   The  software  can scan according to the resolution, size and color mode of the scanning device itself. At the same time, It Increases post-processing of
              scanned pictures, including one-click beautification, intelligent correction and text recognition.  Other image processing tips can also be reflected in this software, such as clipping, ro‚Äê
              tation, etc.
