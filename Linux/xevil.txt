NAME
       xevil - An evil game

SYNOPSIS
       xevil [argument-list]

DESCRIPTION
       XEvil is a 3rd person, side-view, fast-action, kill-them-before-they-kill-you game. Fight against either computer-controlled enemies, or another player.

       You  sinned in life. And now you must pay. Satan pits the recently deceased against each other to fight for rank in Hell. Your skill determines your fate for all eternity. This contest is known as
       XEvil.
