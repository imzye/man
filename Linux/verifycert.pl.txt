NAME
         verifycert.pl

SYNOPSIS
         A simple script to verify X509 and GSI certificates.

         verifycert.pl [-q] [-s] -cert <path to cert.pem> [ -cadir /path/to/CA/dir ] [ -cafile /path/to/a/CA.pem ]
