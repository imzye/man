NAME
       previsat - satellite tracking software for observing purposes

SYNOPSIS

       previsat

DESCRIPTION
       previsat is very easy to use, it shows positions of artificial satellites in real-time or manual mode. PreviSat is able to make predictions of their passes and also predictions of Iridium flares.
