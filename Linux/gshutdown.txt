NAME
       gshutdown - An avanced shutdown utility

SYNOPSIS
       gshutdown

DESCRIPTION
       GShutdown is an advanced shutdown utility, which allows you to schedule the shutdown or the restart of your computer.

       With it, you can simply and quickly choose the turn off time.

AUTHOR
       GShutdown was written by GShutdown Team <gshutdown@gmail.com>
