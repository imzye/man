NAME
       flood_redir6

SYNOPSIS
       flood_redir6 [-HFD] interface [target] [oldrouter [newrouter]]

       Flood the local network with ICMPv6 redirect packets.  -F/-D/-H add fragment/destination/hopbyhop header to bypass simple filters -a adds hopbyhop with router alert
