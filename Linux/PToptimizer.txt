NAME
       PToptimizer - Wrapper around control point optimization routines

SYNOPSIS
       PToptimizer script_file

DESCRIPTION
       PToptimizer is a simple wrapper around control point optimization routines available in libpano13.

SEE ALSO
       <http://wiki.panotools.org/PTOptimizer>
