NAME
       veyon-auth-helper - Veyon PAM Authentication Helper

SYNOPSIS
       veyon-auth-helper

DESCRIPTION
       veyon-auth-helper is a PAM-su authentication helper for the Veyon service (called veyon-service).

       NOTE: veyon-auth-helper has to be installed setuid root and must be available in your system's environment $PATH.

OPTIONS
       No options present.

SEE ALSO
       ica(1),italc(1),imc(8)

       http://veyon.io/
