NAME
     obtautomer — enumerate tautomer SMILES and canonical tautomer SMILES

SYNOPSIS
     obtautomer [-c] filename

DESCRIPTION
     Enumerates tautomer SMILES and canonical tautomer SMILES.

EXAMPLES
           obtautomer molecule.smi > tautomers.smi

     Enumerates tautomers for molecule.smi and writes them to tautomers.smi
