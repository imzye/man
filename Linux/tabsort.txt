NAME
       tabsort - Sort a FITS binary table based on values in one column.

SYNOPSIS
       tabsort [options] column-name input-file output-file

OPTIONS
       -d     Sort in descending order (default: ascending)

AUTHOR
       The Astrometry.net team. Principal investigators are David W. Hogg (NYU) and Dustin Lang (CMU).
