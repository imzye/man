NAME
       gcin-gb-toggle - gcin's Chinese conversion toggle tool

SYNOPSIS
       gcin-gb-toggle

DESCRIPTION
       gcin-gb-toggle  toggles  gcin's  output between traditional Chinese and
       simplified Chinese.

SEE ALSO
       gcin(1).
