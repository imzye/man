NAME
       ll2utm - Convert Latitude and Longitude to UTM coordinates.

SYNOPSIS
       ll2utm latitude longitude

       Latitude and longitude are in decimal degrees.  Use negative for south or west.

DESCRIPTION
       ll2utm  converts Latitude and Longitude to UTM coordinates.

OPTIONS
       None.

EXAMPLES
       ll2utm 42.619 -71.34717

       zone = 19T, easting = 307504, northing = 4721177

SEE ALSO
       More detailed information is in the PDF files at https://github.com/wb2osz/direwolf/tree/master/doc/.
