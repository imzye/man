glaxium
       glaxium - 3d spaceship shoot-em-up

SYNOPSIS
       glaxium [options]

DESCRIPTION
       This  manual  page briefly documents glaxium command.  This manual page
       was written for the Debian GNU/Linux distribution because the  original
       program does not have a manual page.

       glaxium  is a 3d 'shoot-em-up' game inspired by games such as Space In‐
       vaders and Galaxian.

       The goal is to destroy as many opposing ships as possible while  avoid‐
       ing being destroyed yourself.  The the screen shows your current shield
       status; if it drops to empty, you will  lose.   The  triangles  in  the
       lower-right  hand  side  of the screen show how many times you may fire
       your special weapon.

       Use the following keys to control your ship:

       arrow keys or joystick
              Move the ship

       w, z   Fire the normal weapon

       x      Fire the special weapon (you may fire this only a limited number
              of times)

       p      Pause

       q, Escape
              Quit

       d      Display the current FPS count.

OPTIONS
       All  options begin with a single dash ('-') A summary of options is in‐
       cluded below.

       -f     Run in fullscreen mode.

       -1     Use a resolution of 500x400 pixels.

       -2     Use a resolution of 640x480 pixels.

       -3     Use a resolution of 800x600 pixels.  This is the default.

       -4     Use a resolution of 1024x768 pixels.
