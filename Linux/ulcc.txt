NAME
       ulcc - Teaching children by pictures

DESCRIPTION
       ulcc is an educational tool/game targeted at children. It is designed to aid in learning words through visual samples. As a side function it can also help to familiarize one with a mouse.

       This program is useful for elementary schools.
