NAME
       Clover2 computer language

       cclover2   [source  file(.clcl  or  .cl)]  clover2  [source  file(.cl)]
       iclover2

DESCRIPTION
       cclover2 is the compiler for Clover2. clover2 is  the  Virtual  Machine
       for Clover2. iclover2 is REPL for Clover2.

USAGE
       SEE              https://github.com/ab25cq/clover2/wiki              or
       /usr/share/doc/clover2/*.md files(Japanese or English)
