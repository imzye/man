NAME
       carburetor - Browse anonymously

SYNOPSIS
       carburetor [OPTION…]

DESCRIPTION
       Discover anonymous browsing with Carburetor on your phones and computers. Tailored for GNOME, it's your hidden superhero for online adventures, powered by TOR. Don't get your hands dirty with sys‐
       tem files any more – just tap in the app, keeping your online world safe and private. Customize settings if you want, but you don't have to. Carburetor is Free Software and puts you in control. No
       worries, just enjoy your anonymous browsing!

OPTIONS
       -h, --help - Show help options

       --help-all - Show all help options

       --help-gapplication - Show GApplication options

       --gapplication-service - Enter GApplication service mode (use from D-Bus service files)
