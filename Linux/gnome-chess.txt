NAME
       gnome-chess — A simple chess application for GNOME

SYNOPSIS
       gnome-chess

DESCRIPTION
       gnome-chess is a chess application, previously known as glchess.  It supports both CECP- and UCI-compatible artificial intelligences.

       More information can be found at https://gitlab.gnome.org/GNOME/gnome-chess

SEE ALSO
       gnuchess(6), crafty(6), amy(6), faile(6), xboard(6).
