NAME
       maq_eval.pl - evaluate Maq SNPs/indels

SYNOPSIS
       maq_eval.pl <command> <arguments>

DESCRIPTION
       evaluate Maq SNPs/indels

       Version: 0.1.11

OPTIONS
       sub    evaluate substitutions

       geno   evaluate genotyping results

       indelpe
              evaluate indelpe results

AUTHOR
