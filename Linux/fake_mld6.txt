NAME
       fake_mld6

SYNOPSIS
       fake_mld6 [-l] interface add|delete|query [multicast-address [ttl [own-ip [own-mac-address [destination-mac-address]]]]]

       Ad(d)vertise  or  delete  yourself - or anyone you want - in a multicast group of your choice Query ask on the network who is listening to multicast addresses Use -l to loop and send (in 5s intervals)
       until Control-C is pressed.
