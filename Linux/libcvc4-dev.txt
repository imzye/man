NAME
       SmtEngine - the primary interface to CVC4's theorem-proving capabilities

DESCRIPTION
       SmtEngine is the main entry point into the CVC4 theorem prover API.

SMTENGINE OPTIONS
       The  SmtEngine  is in charge of setting and getting information and options.  Numerous options are available via the SmtEngine::setOption() call.  SmtEngine::setOption() and SmtEngine::getOption()
