NAME
       plasma-prime-select - Secure wrapper for executing prime-select with policykit

SYNOPSIS
       plasma-prime-select

DESCRIPTION
       plasma-prime-select is a wrapper for policykit to execute prime-select for plasma-optimus.  It is not meant to be executed outside of the plasma-optimus plasmoid and will fail if attempted.

USAGE
       Not meant to be executed outside of the plasma-optimus plasmoid

       Erich Eickmeyer.
