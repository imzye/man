NAME
       geophar - Swiss army knife for the math teacher

SYNOPSIS

       geophar [{-h | --help}]

DESCRIPTION
       geophar contains every tool you would like to find when preparing math courses, exercises or their keys.

OPTIONS
       -h, --help
           Show summary of options.
