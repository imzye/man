NAME
       jetring-accept - accept a changeset

SYNOPSIS
       jetring-accept dir changeset

DESCRIPTION
       Accepts a changeset and adds it to the jetring directory "dir", updating the dir/index file, and optionally gpg signing it.

AUTHOR
       Joey Hess, <joey@kitenet.net>.

SEE ALSO
