NAME
       rgfa-findcrisprs - find CRISPRs in assembly graphs

SYNOPSIS
       rgfa-findcrisprs <gfa>

DESCRIPTION
       Finds CRISPRs given in assembly graphs specified by a GFA file. CRISPRs have a structure ARU1RU..RUnRB where |U|~|R| in [24..50].

SEE ALSO
       gfadiff(1), rgfa-mergelinear(1), rgfa-simdebruijn(1)
