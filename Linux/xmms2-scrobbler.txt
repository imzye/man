NAME
       xmms2-scrobbler - Audioscrobbler/Last.FM client for XMMS2

SYNOPSIS
       xmms2-scrobbler

DESCRIPTION
       xmms2-scrobbler sends information about the tracks you play with XMMS2 to the Last.FM service to build a profile of the type of music you listen to and find people that have similar tastes in
       music.

OPTIONS
       There are no options available.

SEE ALSO
       xmms2(1), http://xmms2.xmms.org.
