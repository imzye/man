NAME
       utm2ll - Convert UTM coordinates to Latitude and Longitude.

SYNOPSIS
       utm2ll zone easting northing

              zone is UTM zone 1 thru 60 with optional latitudinal band.

              easting is x coordinate in meters.

              northing is y coordinate in meters.

DESCRIPTION
       ll2utm  converts UTM coordinates to latitude and longitude.

OPTIONS
       None.

EXAMPLES
       utm2ll 19T 306130 4726010

       latitude = 42.662139, longitude = -71.365553
