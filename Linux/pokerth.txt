NAME
       pokerth - Texas hold'em game

SYNOPSIS
       pokerth

DESCRIPTION
       pokerth  is  a  free  implementation  of  the  famous  "Texas  hold'em"  card  game  which  is  often  played  in casinos. If you don't understand the rules of this game then please have a look at
       http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holdem where it is very well documented.

AUTHOR
