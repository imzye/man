NAME
       fvshowyuv - Visualize the YUV color space

SYNOPSIS
       fvshowyuv

DESCRIPTION
       Shows a 2D plane for the YUV colorspace with adjustable Y component. On
       the horizontal axis the U values are shown increasing from left to
       right, on the vertical axis the V values are display increasing from
       bottom to top. With the up and down cursor keys the Y value can be set,
       initially starting at 128.

KEYS
       UP/DOWN
           Increase/decrease Y by 1.

       PAGE-UP/PAGE-DOWN
           Increase/decrease Y by 20.

       q/Esc
           Quit the viewer.

EXAMPLES
           *fvshowyuv*
                  Show YUV plane.
