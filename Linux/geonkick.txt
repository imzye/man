NAME
       geonkick - a percussion synthesizer.

SYNOPSIS
       geonkick <preset file>

DESCRIPTION
       geonkick is a synthesizer that can synthesize elements of percussion.

OPTIONS
       geonkick doesn't any take options.

EXAMPLES
       geonkick /path/to/preset.gkick
