NAME
       Graph::MoreUtils::Line::SelfLoopVertex - marker for artificial self-loops

DESCRIPTION
       With "loop_end_vertices" option Graph::Line adds self-loops on pendant vertices thus preserving them from getting "lost".  Instances of Graph::MoreUtils::Line::SelfLoopVertex are used to represent
       the artificial self-loops in the resulting line graphs.
