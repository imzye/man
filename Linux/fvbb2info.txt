NAME
       fvbb2info - Show information about Bumblebee 2 camera.

SYNOPSIS
       fvbb2info

DESCRIPTION
       Open the first available Bumblebee 2 camera on the IEEE1394 bus and
       show information about the internal settings and values.

EXAMPLES
       fvbb2info
           Open camera and show info.
