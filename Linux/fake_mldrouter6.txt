NAME
       fake_mldrouter6

SYNOPSIS
       fake_mldrouter6 [-l] interface advertise|solicitate|terminate [own-ip [own-mac-address]]

       Announce, delete or soliciated MLD router - sourself or others.  Use -l to loop and send (in 5s intervals) until Control-C is pressed.
