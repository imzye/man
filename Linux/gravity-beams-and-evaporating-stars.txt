NAME
       gravity-beams-and-evaporating-stars - a game about hurling asteroids into the sun

SYNOPSIS
       gravity-beams-and-evaporating-stars

DESCRIPTION
       In  Gravity  Beams  and  Evaporating Stars: The Game you take control of a lone planet whose star is dying.  Use your gravity beams to hurl nearby asteroids into the sun, feeding it some extra matter.
       While saving the star, be sure to avoid being hit by the asteroids yourself.
