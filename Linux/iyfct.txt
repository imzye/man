NAME
       iyfct - In Your Face City Trains v 1.0.2

SYNOPSIS
       Side scrolling endless runner game

INSTRUCTIONS
       The  goal  of  the  game is to survive as long as you can.  Jump over trains with closed doors and try (as much as possible) to run through trains with open doors to avoid birds and tunnels. Some open
       trains will give you coffee. When your coffee-meter is full, you have one extra life.

CONTROLS
       Space  Jump

       R      Restart

       1-4,F1-F4
              Set zoom

       P      Pause game

       M      Mute sound

DESCRIPTION
       This game is made with LOVE. You can find more games and information about the author(s) at http://tangramgames.dk/

OPTIONS
       -h, --help Show manpage.
